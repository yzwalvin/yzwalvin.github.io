Developing Purr-suit of Home

As a Product Manager and Programmer, I led a team of 5 to design and develop a 2D game in C++ inspired by "Crossy Road," utilizing DigiPen's Alpha Engine to implement core gameplay mechanics and features.

I designed and implemented the core architecture and mechanics of the project while coordinating with team members to ensure smooth integration of features and maintain overall project cohesion.
