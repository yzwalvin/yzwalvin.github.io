Sep 2023 - Nov 2023

You play as a prisoner, trying to escape from the prison "PlaceHolder".

Avoid the guards and their field of vision to not get caught, while navigating through the prison, and using abilities to aid your escape.

Official Trailer

Developing

As a Product Manager and Programmer, I led a team of 4 to design and develop a 2D game in C, utilizing DigiPen's CProcessing Engine to implement core gameplay mechanics and features.

I implemented useful utility functions, flash ability, created custom sounds and artwork, fog of war feature, and also worked closely with team members to coordinate a cohesive game.
